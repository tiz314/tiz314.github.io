GNSS architectures are continuously challenged by external signal interference and malicious threats. But how do different commercial chipsets actually hold up when the signals turn malicious?

Together with @[Rebel-Nightmare](https://github.com/Rebel-Nightmare), @[mightygibbon](https://github.com/mightygibbon), and @[LeiCap](https://github.com/LetiCap), we recently concluded an exploration into the resilience of modern positioning systems. 

We tested various commercial chipsets under different scenarios, specifically focusing on performance disparities and their behavior during simulated spoofing attacks. The results highlight a massive gap in resilience across the industry.

**Dive into the data and read the full report [here](https://github.com/void-antani-int/GNSS_Robustness)**!
